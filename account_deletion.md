# Request Account Deletion
**App Name:** Calmeals
**Developer:** Tilotham

## How to Delete Your Account
We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so using one of the following methods:

### Option 1: Delete within the App (Recommended)
This is the fastest and most secure way to delete your data.
1. Open the **Calmeals** app.
2. Go to the **Profile** page.
3. Tap **Delete**.
4. Confirm the action. Your account and personal data will be deleted in 30 days.

### Option 2: Request via Email
If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion via email.
**Security Note:** To prevent unauthorized deletion, we strictly require identity verification.

1. Send an email to **[tilothamdhanabal@gmail.com](mailto:tilothamdhanabal@gmail.com)**.
2. **IMPORTANT:** The email **MUST be sent from** the same email address registered to the Calmeals account. Requests from different email addresses will be rejected to protect user privacy.
3. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request - Calmeals"**.
4. We may reply to ask for additional confirmation (e.g., specific account details or recent purchase ID) before processing.
5. Once verified, we will delete your data within 7 days.

## Data Retention Policy
When your account is deleted, the following data is properly removed from our systems:
- Personal Profile (Email, Name, Age, Height, Weight)
- All Food & Calorie Logs
- User Preferences and Settings

**Note on Subscriptions:** Deleting your account *does not* automatically cancel your Google Play Subscription. You must manage your subscriptions via the Google Play Store.
